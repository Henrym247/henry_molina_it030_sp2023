World Series MVP

The World Series Most Valuable Player Award was first given out to the best player on the World Series winning team starting 1955. Therefore, seasons prior to 1955 there were no World Series MVPs.

1956: Don Larson

1958: Bob Turley

1961: Whitey Ford

1962: Ralph Terry

1977: Reggie Jackson

1978: Bucky Dent

1996: John Wetteland

1998: Scott Brosius
