Timeline

1900s

Jan 1903: Frank Farrell and Bill Devery acquire the Baltimore franchise of the American League for $18,000 and migrate the team to Manhattan.

Mar 1903: Team will play in all-wood park at 168th Street and Broadway. Team will be known as the Highlanders and their home park Hilltop Park.

Apr. 1903: The Highlanders play their first game; they lose to Washington 3-1.

1910s

Apr. 1912: The Highlanders first use the pinstripes uniform, the start of the iconic team uniform.

Apr. 1913: The Highlanders renamed to the Yankees, after moving to the Polo Grounds.

Jan 1915: Col. Jacob Ruppert and Col. Tillinghast L’Hommedieu Huston acquire the Yankees for $1.25 million.

1920s

Jan 1920: Yankees purchase the contract of Babe Ruth from the Boston Red Sox.

May 1922: Construction for a new ballpark Yankee Stadium begins.

May 1922: Col. Ruppert buys out Col. Huston.

Apr 1923: First game in Yankee Stadium is a win against the Red Sox 4-1.

Sept 1927: Babe Ruth sets MLB single season homerun record at 60.

1930s

Jul 1934: Babe Ruth hits his 700th homerun.

Jul 1934: Yankees buy Joe DiMaggio from the San Francisco Seals.

Jul 1939: Yankees retire Lou Gehrig’s number and makes his famous speech.

1940s

Jan 1945: Dan topping, Del Webb, and Larry MacPhail buy the Yankees for $2.8 million from the estate of late Col. Ruppert.

June 1948: Babe Ruth number is retired.

1950s

Apr 1951: Mickey Mantle makes his debut in a 4-0 win against Boston.

Dec 1951: Joe DiMaggio retires from baseball.

Oct 1956: Don Larson pitches the only perfect game is World Series history against the Brooklyn Dodgers.

1960s

Oct 1961: Roger Maris breaks the single season homerun record with 61.

Nov 1964: CBS buys 80 percent of the Yankees for $11.2 million, later buying the last 20.

June 1969: Mickey Mantle number is retired.

1970s

Aug 1972: Yankees sign a lease to play in a remodeled Yankee Stadium to be completed in 1976.

Jan 1973: A limited partnership, lead by George M. Steinbrenner III, buy the Yankees from CBS.

1974-75: The Yankees play their home games in Shea Stadium (home the Mets) due to the remodeling.

Apr 1976: First game back in Yankee Stadium since the remodeling, Yankees win over the Minnesota Twins.

Nov 1976: Reggie Jackson is signed by the Yankees.

1980s

Dec 1980: Yankees sign Dave Winfield to a record breaking 10-year contract.

Dec 1987: Yankees sign a 12-year television contract with Madison Square Garden Network.

1990s

Aug 1993: Reggie Jackson number is retired.

Jan 1997: Don Mattingly announces his retirement.

May 1998: David Wells tosses a perfect game.

Jul 1999: David Cone tosses a perfect game.

2000s

Oct 2000: Yankees win the World Series against the New York Mets.

Sept. 2008: The Yankees Play their last game in Yankee Stadium.

Apr 2009: The Yankees play their first game in the new and current Yankee Stadium.

Sept 2009: Derek Jeter passes Lou Gehrig as the most hits as a Yankee at 2,721 hits.

2010s

Jul 2011: Derek Jeter gets his 3000th hit becoming the first Yankee to reach 3000 hits.

Sept 2011: Mariano Rivera breaks all time saves record at 602 saves.

2020s

Oct 2022: Aaron Judge breaks Roger Maris American League single season homerun record with 62 homeruns.
